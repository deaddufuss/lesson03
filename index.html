what is pokemon?

(a quick summary of pokemon) pokemon is an RPG game where you play as a trainer. you start off by geting a starter pokemon that ether has the type fire, water or grass. with you're new pokemon you can explore the region and catch pokemon in pokeballs. while on you're pokemon journey you will have to battle gym leaders to get badges to compete in the pokemon league in the pokemon league you will have to defeat all of the elite four after that you will become the pokemon master
